Cat's are so great I am so happy to have a blog all about cats. They are my favorite animal and this website was in no way created just so Gabbie could demonstrate some popular hacking technique. No way at all. I just have a simple love for cats.

Did you know that cats walk like camels and giraffes? They move both of their right feet first, then move both of their left feet. No other animals walk this way. By the way if you are reading this part then you are very attentive! Also, did you know that male cats are actually more likely to be left-pawed? Female cats are more likely to be right-pawed.
